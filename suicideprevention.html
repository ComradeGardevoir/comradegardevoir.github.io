SUICIDE PREVENTION TIPS

Get help

Suicide is the forever decision. It causes mourning by those who knew the victim.

GET HELP

If you know someone who has suicidal thoughts, please contact a trusted adult or the suicide helpline.

THINK ABOUT IT

If you are thinking about suicide, contact the National Suicide Prevention Lifeline at 1-800-273-8255. Get help immediately.
